# Kafka Platform Overview

The platform team runs three Kafka clusters: prod-events, prod-logs, and
staging. Topics are provisioned through Terraform in the infra-kafka repo;
direct topic creation in production is disabled.

## Retention and quotas
Default retention is 7 days; prod-logs is 3 days. Producer quotas are enforced
per service account. Consumer lag alerts page the platform on-call when lag
exceeds 100k messages for 15 minutes.
