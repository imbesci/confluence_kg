# Deploy Process

All services deploy through the CI pipeline in GitHub Actions. Merges to main
trigger a canary deploy to 5% of traffic for 30 minutes before full rollout.

## Rollbacks
Rollbacks use the deploy bot in #deploys: `/rollback <service> <version>`.
During a SEV-1 incident, the on-call engineer may roll back without review.

## Freeze windows
Deploy freezes apply during Black Friday week and the last two business days
of each quarter. The release manager (currently Dana K.) approves exceptions.
