# On-Call Escalation Policy

The primary on-call engineer is paged first via PagerDuty. If a SEV-1 page is
not acknowledged within 10 minutes, PagerDuty escalates to the secondary
on-call, then to the engineering manager after a further 10 minutes.

## Severity levels
SEV-1 means customer-facing outage. SEV-2 means degraded service with a
workaround. SEV-3 is internal-only impact and is handled in business hours.

## Handoffs
Weekly rotation hands off Mondays at 10:00 ET in the #oncall Slack channel.
The outgoing engineer posts open incidents and any silenced alerts. Platform
team owns the Kafka and Postgres alert policies.
