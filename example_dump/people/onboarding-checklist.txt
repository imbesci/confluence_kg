# Engineering Onboarding Checklist

New engineers get laptop and access provisioning through the IT portal on day
one. Managers assign an onboarding buddy for the first four weeks.

Week 1 covers dev environment setup and the deploy process walkthrough.
Week 2 includes shadowing the on-call rotation and an intro to the incident
response process. All new engineers complete security training in the first
30 days.
