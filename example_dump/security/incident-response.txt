# Security Incident Response

Suspected security incidents are reported in #security-incidents or to
security@example.com. The security on-call triages within 30 minutes.

## Roles
Every SEV-1 security incident gets an incident commander, a comms lead, and a
scribe. Legal is looped in before any external disclosure. Post-incident
reviews are blameless and due within five business days, filed in the
incident-reviews Confluence space.
